Who We Are

ISTAD Online Academy is a leading digital education platform based in Cambodia, dedicated to empowering the next generation of tech professionals. We provide expert-led, project-based courses that bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world industry demands.

Our Story

Founded with a mission to make quality tech education accessible to everyone in Southeast Asia, ISTAD has grown from a small classroom initiative into a full-scale online learning ecosystem. We believe that talent is everywhere — opportunity shouldn't be limited by location or background.

Today, we serve thousands of learners across the region with courses designed and delivered by industry professionals who understand what employers actually need.

🚀

Our Mission

To deliver flexible, high-quality tech education that equips students with the skills and confidence to thrive in the modern digital economy. We combine cutting-edge curriculum with expert mentorship to ensure every learner achieves real, measurable outcomes.

🌏

Our Vision

To become Southeast Asia's most trusted online tech academy, where any motivated individual — regardless of their background — can access world-class learning and launch a successful career in software development, design, or data science.
